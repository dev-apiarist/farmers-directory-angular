About Us


We are a registry company operating locally in Jamaica,
We are committed to helping our users create a avenue to sell their produce to local business.
Our users ranged from small farmers to plantations owners, and the target businesses range from individual buyers, small and medium size companies, large corporation, and investors.

HISTORY

The Farmer Director was founded in 2022 by a group of engineers who have came to the realization that there wasn’t a platform to support our local farmers. Wanted to create a solution for this, they came together as a team to fix the issue. After much debate, the team came to the conclusion to make a register where users (farmer) can register and list their produce, and potential buyers can browse for the produce they need.
